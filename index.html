About Me

DESIGNING
WITH PURPOSE

I'm Isabella, a UX/UI Design student based in New Jersey. I came into this field with little coding experience but a strong belief that good design can make technology feel human. That belief drives everything I create.

I want to work with startups and small businesses — teams that have real problems to solve and need a designer who can research, think, and build with intention. I am interested in the full design process: from understanding users to delivering polished, production-ready interfaces.

I use AI as part of my workflow — not as a shortcut, but as a tool I direct. Through this course I learned to use VS Code, Git, GitHub, and the terminal for the first time. Every step was intentional. Every decision was mine.

How I Work

MY DESIGN
PROCESS

Every project follows a structured approach — from research to high-fidelity delivery.

01
Discover
User interviews, competitive analysis, and defining the real problem before jumping to solutions.
02
Define
Synthesizing research into personas, journey maps, and a clear problem statement.
03
Design
Wireframes → design system → high-fidelity UI with constant feedback loops throughout.
04
Deliver
Interactive prototypes, developer handoff specs, and usability testing to validate.
