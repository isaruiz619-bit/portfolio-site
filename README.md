## Reflection
When I first read this assignment, I was a little worried because I have no experience building a website. The instructions felt complicated at first, especially when they mentioned specs, sprints, and preventing drift. Since I am still new to all of this, it was intimidating in the beginning. However, after breaking the assignment down step by step and getting guidance to better understand what was actually being asked, the process became much clearer.

I started by creating a repository and adding a simple HTML file. Once I enabled GitHub Pages and saw the site go live, I began to feel more comfortable and less overwhelmed. I started realizing that I could manage it by taking it one step at a time.

What I learned most from this project is that structure really matters. Instead of randomly clicking buttons, I understood the importance of moving carefully and intentionally through each stage. Even though I began this experience feeling intimidated, I now understand the basic flow of deploying a website and feel more confident navigating the process.
